# How-Much-Can-You-Borrow-Against-Bitcoin-2026
Find out how much you can borrow against Bitcoin in 2026. Learn LTV ratios, borrowing limits, and safe strategies to maximize liquidity without liquidation risk.

---

## Introduction

Your borrowing power depends on a single critical metric: Loan-to-Value (LTV).

---

## What Is LTV?

LTV = Loan Amount ÷ Collateral Value

---

## Borrowing Ranges

- 20–30% → Safe  
- 40–50% → Moderate  
- 60–70% → High risk  

---

## Example

BTC value: $100,000  

| LTV | Loan |
|-----|------|
| 30% | $30,000 |
| 50% | $50,000 |
| 70% | $70,000 |

---

## Factors Affecting Borrowing Power

- Market volatility  
- Platform limits  
- Risk tolerance  

---

## Why You Should Borrow Less Than Maximum

Borrowing max LTV increases liquidation risk significantly.
